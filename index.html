Manufacturing Consulting Services

At EEB Engineering, we are specialized in providing manufacturing consultancy services that include operations such as facing, turning, grooving, parting, threading, knurling, reaming, boring, tapping, drilling, cutting (tubes & rods).

Our consultancy team is composed of experienced engineers and industry professionals who provide customized solutions to address the distinctive requirements of our clients in a variety of industries. We help businesses enhance their manufacturing operations from design to production by leveraging our proficiency in precision machining and fabrication. We offer comprehensive support to guarantee optimal performance and cost-effectiveness, whether you need assistance with machine selection, process optimization, fabrication strategies, or quality control measures.

We enable our clients to achieve increased productivity, superior product quality, and shorter lead times by incorporating industry best practices and advanced technology. We are a dependable partner in the development of manufacturing capabilities and the promotion of business expansion due to our dedication to innovation and excellence.

Prototyping Consultancy Services (3D Printing Machines)

3D printing consultancy services offer tailored solutions for the entire prototyping process, from design to production.

Hydroponics Consultancy Services

We provide comprehensive hydroponics consultancy services designed to transform agricultural practices by implementing sustainable and innovative solutions. Our team of professionals is committed to assisting clients in utilizing the potential of hydroponics to help the new age agripreneurs by providing the right support and resources to achieve greater revenues.

We offer a complete solution for large hydroponic farms, greenhouses, and smaller units for homes or institutions. Our expertise brings in feasibility studies, farm design and site planning, technology innovation, project management, agronomy support, farm operation, and maintenance.

We enable our clients to cultivate high-yield crops in controlled environments, minimizing water usage and maximizing productivity, by incorporating cutting-edge technology and proven methodologies. Our hydroponics consultancy endeavours to revolutionize agriculture by providing solutions that are both ecologically sound and economically viable, with a focus on precision and sustainability.
