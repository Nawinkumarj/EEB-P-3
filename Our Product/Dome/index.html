About Us

A geodesic dome is a spherical architectural structure made of interconnected triangular frames. Unveiling our cutting-edge 6V geodesic dome project, an architectural marvel that redefines design, functionality, and sustainability. This innovative structure employs a sophisticated system of connecting struts, forming triangular frames that maximise stability and resilience.

With ease of assembly, exceptional durability, and the versatility to suit various applications, our geodesic domes represent the perfect harmony of science and art. Domes are used in various applications such as event spaces, sports facilities, greenhouses, residential or commercial buildings, and industrial storage due to their structural strength, efficient use of space, aesthetic appeal, eco-friendliness, and cost-effectiveness.

Frame Material

The frame is constructed using galvanised steel (GI steel), selected for its durability and suitability (GI steel size is based on the dome size).

Dome Covering

A tensile fabric is used to cover the dome, providing strength and flexibility. This fabric also includes a transparent fabric feature for natural light.

Insularion

To ensure proper insulation, a composite material is employed. It consists of: I. Fabric for an outer protective layer interior aesthetic (inner padding: aluminium thermal sheet and fabric) II. Aluminium sheets to enhance durability and heat resistance.

Key Features
Effortless Assembly and Disassembl

Each strut and fastener in the dome’s structure is meticulously engineered for simplicity and precision. This modular system ensures that the dome can be assembled and disassembled with ease, making it an ideal choice for both permanent installations and temporary setups.

Budget-Conscious Builders

Geodesic domes are a cost-effective solution for builders, as their efficient design uses fewer materials, reducing both construction costs and time. This streamlined approach results in strong, durable structures at a lower overall expense.

Aesthetic Versatility

The geodesic dome can be customised with a variety of finishes, colours, and materials to suit your aesthetic preferences. From transparent panels for maximum natural light to opaque materials for privacy, the dome can be tailored to meet your vision
