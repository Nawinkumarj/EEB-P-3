About Us

About Us

A Floating Leisure is a recent development by our expert engineers uniquely designed using barrels to offer a distinctive and luxurious experience to all the end users. The floating leisure gives an adventurous thrill to the guests while giving the comfort of a hotel. This is designed to float on water bodies such as lakes, rivers, and calm seas. Guests can enjoy glass-bottomed floors, underwater rooms, and various water-based activities, enhancing their stay with additional entertainment and creating lasting memories.

The floating leisure is buoyant, allowing it to float. Additionally, it facilitates mobility between different destinations, offering a dynamic perspective and varied locations. This floating hotel can afford maximum space that also includes features such as guest rooms with water views, a central lobby, a dining area, recreational facilities, and outdoor decks. All the guests can enjoy panoramic views of the picturesque landscape and their surroundings from the comfort of their seating on the floating leisure.
