1-Year Warranty

We offer a 1-year warranty that covers weld leaks, ensuring that the scaffolding is free from manufacturing defects. However, this warranty does not cover physical damage caused by misuse or external factors.

Motor Warranty

The motor used in the scaffolding system is covered under a one-year warranty. After this period, servicing will incur additional charges for maintenance or repairs.

Safety Rating

Our motorized scaffolding is rated 5 for the safety of the materials used. This high safety rating ensures that the scaffolding system adheres to industry standards for strength and reliability.

Self-Installation

We provide scaffolding systems designed for self-installation, meaning there are no installation charges. The system is user-friendly, allowing workers to quickly and easily set up the scaffolding without the need for professional installers.

Delivery Charges

Delivery charges depend on the distance from the delivery location and are based on the rates shown in the Porter app. These charges will be calculated at the time of purchase.

Delivery Time

We strive to deliver your motorized scaffolding within 2 to 3 weeks from the date of payment. This timeline ensures you receive the product promptly for your project needs.

Weight Capacity

Our motorized scaffolding systems are designed to support a weight capacity of 200-300 kg, making them suitable for both light and heavy-duty tasks.

Rental Availability

Rental options are available for motorized scaffolding, subject to approval from our management. This provides flexibility for customers who need temporary access solutions for short-term projects.
